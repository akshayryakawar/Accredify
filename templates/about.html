Accreditation Framework

What is NBA Criterion 4?

Understanding the national accreditation standard this portal is built to serve.

National Board of Accreditation (NBA)

The National Board of Accreditation (NBA) is an autonomous body under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, that evaluates the quality of technical education programmes. NBA accreditation follows the Outcome-Based Education (OBE) framework and is widely recognised as the gold standard for engineering and polytechnic institutions across India.

Criterion 4 — Students' Performance is one of the most critical evaluation criteria in the NBA assessment process for diploma programmes. It carries significant weightage and directly reflects the institution's ability to produce industry-ready graduates.

Criterion 4 — Sub-Criteria Breakdown

Each sub-criterion evaluates a specific dimension of student performance and institutional outcomes.

4.1

Enrolment Ratio

Measures the ratio of students enrolled against the sanctioned intake. Reflects demand, reputation, and accessibility of the programme.

Weightage: High
4.2

Success Rate in Stipulated Period

Evaluates the percentage of students who pass all subjects without backlogs within the normal programme duration.

Weightage: High
4.3

Academic Performance – First Year

Assesses pass percentage in first-year examinations including students with backlogs, indicating foundational academic strength.

Weightage: Medium
4.4

Academic Performance – Second Year

Evaluates academic results of second-year students, tracking progression and improvement from the first year.

Weightage: Medium
4.5

Placement, Higher Studies & Entrepreneurship

Tracks career outcomes of graduates — including job placements, students pursuing higher education, and entrepreneurship ventures.

Weightage: High
4.6

Placement and Higher Studies

Detailed evaluation of student placement statistics including company names, packages offered, and higher education admissions.

Weightage: High
4.7

Professional Activities

Covers faculty and student professional activities — publications in technical magazines, conference participation, and professional society memberships.

Weightage: Medium
About This System

Accredify – Project Overview

This portal is a Django-based Automated Student Performance Data and Report Management System developed specifically for Sandip Polytechnic to streamline NBA Criterion 4 documentation and reporting.

The system replaces manual, error-prone spreadsheet workflows with a centralized, secure, and intelligent platform that allows authorized faculty and administrators to record student data, update academic results, maintain placement records, and generate NBA-compliant reports with a single click.
